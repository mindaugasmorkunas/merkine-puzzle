🥾 HIKER'S REMINDER

📍 Event Details

  • Start Location: Near Merkinė Castle Mound (54.1577, 24.1823)
  • Gathering Time: From 12:00
  • Hike Start: 12:30
  • Distance: 9 KM
  • Duration: Approximately 4 hours
  • Evening Program: Starts at 17:00

📅 Event Agenda

  • At Start: Registration, instructions
  • 1st Rest Stop: Games, hot drinks, plovas, tea, water
  • 2nd Rest Stop: Games, snacks, water, WC
  • Finish: Evening program "Pasaulio puodų" restaurant, hot food, drinks, WC

⚠️ Important Safety Tips

  • Recommended: Bring spare clothes and shoes
  • Bug protection: Use insect repellent and wear appropriate clothing to protect against mosquitoes and ticks
  • Alcohol awareness: Be mindful about alcohol consumption during the hike - stay hydrated and safe

🌟 Equipment Recommendations

  • Check weather conditions - dress accordingly for the day's forecast
  • Comfortable footwear - good hiking boots or shoes with grip, as paths can be wet and slippery
  • Summer clothing - lightweight, breathable clothes suitable for warm weather
  • Sun protection - hat, sunglasses, and sunscreen for sunny summer conditions
  • Spare clothes & shoes (optional) - bring fresh clothes and shoes to change for the evening program if you prefer
  • Accessories - backpack, water bottle, snacks, rain protection
